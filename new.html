HOW IT WORKS

Your Child's Journey With
Concept Tutoring

1

Free Assessment

We meet your child, evaluate their current level, and identify exactly where they need support. Provides parents with clarity on how to progress their child’s learning.

2

Personalised Learning Plan

Every kid learns differently. When needed, we build a tailored program specifically for your child matching their pace, strengths, and goals. Not a shared template.

3

In-Person Sessions

Live, structured sessions of 2–4 hours with an experienced tutor who gets to know your child and adapts every lesson to how they learn.

4

Regular Progress Reports

We track every step of your child’s improvement and share clear, regular reports so you always know exactly how they are progressing.
