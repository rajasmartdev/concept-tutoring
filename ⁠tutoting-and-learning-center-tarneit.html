OUR PHILOSOPHY

In Person Tutoring That Makes a Difference

All sessions are in person for better focus, direct interaction, and a consistent environment. We believe that physical presence creates a stronger connection between tutor and student, allowing for immediate feedback and real-time adjustments to teaching methods.

While our core focus is face-to-face learning, we also provide online classes to reinforce concepts practiced in person, ensuring continuous support even from home.

GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, while our primary focus is in-person learning for better engagement, we do offer online classes to reinforce concepts and provide flexibility when needed.

What age groups do you tutor?

We tutor students from Kindergarten through to Year 10, covering all crucial stages of their primary and early secondary education.

Do you follow the school curriculum?

Absolutely. Our structured approach is designed to align with and support what your child is currently learning in their school curriculum.

How often should my child attend tutoring?

This depends on the individual student's needs, but typically we recommend weekly sessions to maintain consistency and build strong foundational skills.

How many students are in each session?

We keep our groups small — a maximum of 6 students per session — so every child receives focused attention and personalised support from their tutor.

Do you offer a free assessment before enrolling?

Yes! We offer a free initial assessment to understand your child's current level, identify any gaps, and recommend the most suitable program for their needs.
